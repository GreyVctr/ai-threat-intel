# AI Shield Intelligence
## Executive One-Pager

### The Problem
AI systems are increasingly targeted by adversarial attacks, model theft, data poisoning, and prompt-based exploitation. Most threat tooling evolves in academic or open research communities long before it appears in traditional cybersecurity feeds—leaving organizations unprepared. New attack vectors emerge weekly from research papers and security conferences, but organizations lack visibility until after threats are weaponized.

### The Solution
**AI Shield Intelligence** is a specialized external threat intelligence program that identifies emerging threats to AI systems before they impact production environments. We bridge the gap between fast-moving academic research, adversarial tooling, and enterprise security operations by systematically monitoring 50+ academic sources and 30+ industry channels daily.

### How It Works
```
Academic/Industry Sources → Collection & Ingestion → NLP Classification & 
Enrichment → Threat Analysis & Risk Scoring → Distribution (Alerts, 
Briefings, API, SIEM Integration) → Security & AI Teams
```

**Key Capabilities:**
- Automated monitoring of academic papers, conferences, GitHub PoCs, and threat-sharing forums
- NLP classification, entity extraction, and knowledge graph enrichment
- Threat modeling aligned to MITRE ATLAS + AI-specific attack taxonomies
- Multi-channel distribution: real-time alerts, weekly briefings, dashboards, REST API, SIEM/SOAR integration

### Value Proposition
- **Proactive Defense**: Discover threats before widespread exploitation
- **Research-to-Operations Gap Closed**: Converts academic discoveries into operational guidance
- **Reduced Risk**: Prevent AI system compromise, model leakage, and safety failures
- **Faster Decision Cycles**: Actionable intelligence instead of raw research
- **Time Savings**: Eliminate manual monitoring of dozens of disparate sources
